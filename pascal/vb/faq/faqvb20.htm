Q: Как отключить курсор мыши?
A (Crew):

Private Declare Function ShowCursor Lib "user32" (ByVal bShow As Long) As Long

Private Sub cmdOFF_Click()
Call ShowCursor(False)
End Sub

Private Sub cmdON_Click()
Call ShowCursor(True)
End Sub
