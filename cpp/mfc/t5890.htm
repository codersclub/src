The buttons ("plus" and "minus" symbols) are sized
by the CTreeCtrl (window class WC_TREEVIEW) based
upon the verticle hight of the imagelist. If you supply
no imagelist, then then a default size is used.
I know of know way to relationship that the window
makes between the the buttons and the imagelist (or lack of
an imagelist).
