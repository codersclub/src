IMPLEMENT_OLETYPELIB and DECLARE_OLETYPELIB get the library's GUID (the 1st
one in the .odl or .idl file). 

Also, I needed to override CCmdTarget's GetDispatchIID to return the
dispinterface GUID.

Finally, in a program I recently wrote, I needed to add the following:
	AfxOleRegisterTypeLib(AfxGetInstanceHandle(), , ); 
to actually register the type lib with the Windows.
I put the AfxOleRegisterTypeLib statement right after
COleObjectFactory::UpdateRegistryAll() in my InitInstance method.
