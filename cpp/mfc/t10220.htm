Hi,

You haven't stated where r u using the CListCtrl, and how r u sending
the edit commands to your application. But one cause to the your problem
could be that u have accelerators for the edit commands common
shortcuts, and
that your applications handle those commands differently.
If this is the case, simply check (when handling the edit commands)
whether
you are editing a label or editing your stuff, and call the correct
methods for
each case.
