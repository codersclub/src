Mike -- dodd@wilmington.net
Tuesday, March 05, 1996

I had my entire makefile corrupted somehow.  One fine morning I 
started up and it told me that the makefile was NOT created by 
Developer's Studio and that it would create a wrapper for me.  
The wrapper was just about worthless.  I didn't bother writing 
to this group because I didn't think it was MFC related, so my 
solution was to recreate the project, drag/drop the relative 
resources, trap the messages (for classwizard's sanity), copy 
the .h and .cpp files over.  It took an afternoon but it worked 
and I got the rare pleasure of weeding through and rethinking a 
project from the start.  

I don't know what happened to the makefile, I hope it does not 
happen again.  If there's an easier way of reviving a 
'corrupted' makefile please let us know.
