I don't think you can say common controls are 'built-in' the OS.  They are 
stored in a DLL named COMCTL32, which is distributed with Win95, but may be 
distributed freely.  A Win32s version of COMCTL32.DLL is available in the 
\WIN32S\REDIST directory of VC++ CD.
