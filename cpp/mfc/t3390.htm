-----From: "John Elsbree" 

Jim Bucciferro asks:
> When is VC++ 4.1 coming out?

Of course the real answer to this question is, "When it's ready."

The schedule for subscription releases provides for a new release about every 
four months. With the four releases done so far (2.0, 2.1, 2.2, 4.0), 
Microsoft has established a pretty good track record for keeping to this 
schedule, within a month or so. Extrapolate, and you'll have a good ballpark 
estimate.

Don't forget to renew your subscription. If you subscribed prior to 2.1, you 
won't get 4.1 at all, unless you've renewed.
