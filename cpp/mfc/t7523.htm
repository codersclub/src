This will work.  All you're doing is extending the message dispatch mechanism
yourself, and that's just fine.  The thing to remember is that you can't
expect to receive messages themselves directly.  You're not really receiving
messages--you're just getting OnCmdMsg() calls when some other window decides
not to handle a message.
