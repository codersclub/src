RotWalk = IRotView + RotClean 

Dicky Singh -- Dicky@landmark.com
Friday, August 23, 1996

Environment: Win95 MSDEV 4.2 / MSVC++ 4.2


I just uploaded a zipped application RotWalk.ZIP (8K) that lists objects 
that can be identified by a moniker and that are currently running (if any) 
on the system, similar to Microsoft's IRotView.EXE utility (supplied with 
MSDEV and Win32 SDK).
In addition to that, RotWalk.EXE also allows cleaning (remove invalid 
Monikers) from the system Running Object Table.
