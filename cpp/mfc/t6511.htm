NT 4. (The height is the problem, widths are OK).

It seems like the right combination of GetTextMetrics, 
GetSystemMetric( SM_CYDLGFRAME ), etc. should give me the answer.
Anyone know the magic combination?

The combo box in Win95 and NT 4 is displayed with 3D boarder. That is why 
it looks too small when it is just find in NT3.51. I would check the 
combo box size, and the old text metrix before changing its font and/or 
resizing it so that I could record the additional space which is needed 
under each environment. Then you can use that value to properly resize 
your combo box.
