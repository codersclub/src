AD: Call for MFC Beta testers... 

Scot -- Scot@STINGSOFT.COM
Monday, January 20, 1997


Dear MFC Developer,

Stingray Software has a new product entering beta and
we are actively looking for beta testers.  The product
is called Objective Diagram and it lets MFC developers
easily add diagramming (e.g. Visio-like interfaces)
to MFC applications.  Since MFC has very weak GDI encapsulation,
we feel this product fills a very important need in
the MFC community.  Unfortunately we can not reveal the
entire feature list at this point, but you should have a 
general idea if this is something you need and can help us
test before release.

If you are interested in learning more about the product
and applying the beta, you can surf to:

http://www.stingsoft.com or directly to the Objective Diagram beta
sign up at http://www.stingsoft.com/products/obj_diagram/oddir.htm

We look forward to your feedback on the product,

The Objective Diagram Team
Stingray Software, Inc.
http://www.stingsoft.com
