If I got you right, 'initializer' is a separate application, right?
Than, when you call InitializeServices from the DLL, AfxSocketInit() is
called
(and thus initialize sockets usage) for the 'initializer' application,
not for the 
'userapp' application, because DLL functions are working in the process
space of the 
calling application.
You have to call your InitializeService from the userapp.exe.

Hope this helps.
