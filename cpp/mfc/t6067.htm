>     Enviroment: VC++ 4.0 and Windows NT 3.51
>     
>     When you float a control bar, a new bar is drawn with caption and 
>     border. I would like the floating bar to have a caption but without 
>     the thick frame. Could this be done from within a derived CDialogBar 
>     object?

Yes.

Be aware that you must call Detach() from your CDialogBar derivative's
destructor, even if you create your CDialogBar derivative on the stack.
The base class functions do not remove the HWND from afxMapHWND (map of
HWND's to associated CWnd*).
