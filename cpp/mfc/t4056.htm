As a quick "find-in-files" would have told you, 
AddToRecentFileList is called in one place (two places on a mac):
CDocument::SetPathName()
CDocument::OnOpenDocument() (macintosh version).

As these are both virtual, override them two methods and call a custom
MyAddToRecentFileList, which takes a CDocument pointer. 

STATIC_DOWNCAST(CMyApp,AfxGetApp())->
   MyAddToRecentFileList(lpszPathName, this);
