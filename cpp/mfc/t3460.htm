     I think that MSDN is wrong.  I saw a thread on the WINEXT forum 
     (SourceSafe section) recently where the product manager (David 
     Streams) said basically that it's not generally available because it 
     would be too difficult to support (partly because it's not bulletproof 
     and it sounded like time isn't going to be spent on improving it).  
     Also, he asked opinions about using OLE Automation for implementing 
     this API-like behavior which were generally positive, but he couldn't 
     give a date as to when it might be available.  He wanted to cooperate 
     wherever possible, so you might contact him and ask about getting a 
     copy.  He said that the major IDE developer vendors have been given a 
     copy of the specification.  Anyway, depending on your application, you 
     may be able to get a copy.
